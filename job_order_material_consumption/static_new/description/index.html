Product Actual Consumption on Job Order with Stock Warehouse Inventory

This app allows you to see actual Material Consumption of Job Order / Work Order from Stock Warehouse Inventory.

Main Features:

  • Your inventory staff can choose job order and type during inventory picking and transfer so it can be linked with your Job Orders / Work Order / Tasks.
  • Your team can have a real quantity of products you are consuming for job orders / work orders from stock Inventory.
  • Material / Product actual consumptions on Job Order.
